    About Us

    Med Nearby is a cutting-edge, real-time medicine availability tracker designed to make healthcare more accessible and efficient. Our platform empowers users to quickly search for medicines by name and instantly locate their availability in nearby pharmacies. Whether you're looking for a specific drug or checking its stock at local stores, Med Nearby connects you with the information you need at your fingertips. With our seamless integration of real-time data, users can also check the distances to pharmacies and make informed decisions. Med Nearby is dedicated to improving access to essential medicines and enhancing the healthcare experience for all.
